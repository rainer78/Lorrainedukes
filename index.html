Defining the Brands voice and tone


Sapphire Real Estate Agency is a Georgia-based realtor group focused on demystifying the home buying and selling process.

The challenge

A mood board is a visual representation that captures the essence, style, and overall feel of a brand. To make it engaging for your target audience, consider the following elements:

  1. Colors: Choose a color palette that reflects the brand's personality and resonates with the target audience. For a sophisticated and luxurious realtor brand like Sapphire Realtor, deep blues, elegant grays, and rich metallic accents could work well.
  2. Typography: Select fonts that convey professionalism, trustworthiness, and elegance. Opt for clean, modern serif or sans-serif fonts that are easy to read and have a touch of refinement.
  3. Imagery: Incorporate high-quality visuals that evoke the desired emotions. Show stunning images of luxury homes, upscale interior designs, and serene landscapes to evoke a sense of aspiration, comfort, and exclusivity.
  4. Lifestyle: Include images that showcase the desired lifestyle associated with Sapphire Realtor. This could include photos of affluent individuals enjoying their homes, engaging in leisure activities, or entertaining guests in luxurious settings. Such visuals will help your audience imagine themselves living the high-end lifestyle your brand represents.
  5. Tone of Voice: Use text snippets or keywords that represent the desired tone of voice for Sapphire Realtor. Words like "prestige," "elegance," "exclusive," "trustworthy," and "tailored" can be incorporated to guide the brand's messaging.
  6. Logos and Branding Elements: Include the Sapphire Realtor logo, along with other branding elements like icons or patterns that align with the overall visual style.

My Role

I had the incredible opportunity to collaborate on a comprehensive branding project for the esteemed Sapphire Realtor Brand. Drawing upon meticulous research and a deep understanding of their vision, I crafted a captivating mood board that exquisitely captures the brand's distinctive voice and tone.
